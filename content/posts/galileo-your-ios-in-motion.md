[Galileo, Your iOS in Motion!](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/449163977/galileo-your-ios-in-motion) This is the name of a great product which is found on kickstarter.com, the site for funding great ideas.

The Galileo is a motorized iPhone stand which can be controlled from another iOS device like iPhone or iPad. Now you can control your iPhone remotely. This is great during video conference or other photography projects. Check out the video !
