# Welcome to Alex Blog

A tinkerer's notebook since **2009**. This is where I park the things I build, break, fix, and want to remember — from soldering sensors at 2 a.m. to chasing the perfect water-drop photo. If you like electronics, home automation, radio, photography, and a healthy dose of "let's see if this works," you're in the right place.

It's part blog, part archive, part lab journal. Some posts are years old, some are kept alive on purpose. Browse around — there's a good chance something here sparks your next project.

> [!tip] Things I'm building now
>
> - 🚗 **[TripChunker](https://tripchunker.com)** — an iOS app that plans road trips to an event by working *backwards* from your arrival date, splitting the drive into comfortable daily legs with overnight stops and cost estimates. Plan the destination, not the logistics.
> - 🧩 **[SkillsManager](https://skillsmanager.vdsar.net)** — a native macOS app that pulls all your scattered AI-agent config files (CLAUDE.md, .cursorrules, skills) into one place to edit, search, and manage.
